We Are True Vine

True Vine Soul Winning Network is a vibrant community of passionate Christian believers who have come together to share the message of salvation and God's love. Founded on the principles of faith, love, and service, we are committed to making a positive impact on individuals and communities. Our network is built upon the belief that every soul has infinite worth and deserves to experience the redemptive power of Christ.

Our Vision.

At True Vine Soul Winning Network, we envision a world where the light of God's love shines brightly in every heart, where individuals are empowered to become spiritual beacons, and where communities are transformed through the power of faith. Our vision is a world touched by God's grace, where every soul is won for Christ and experiences the boundless joy of salvation.

Our Mission.

Our mission is to be faithful stewards of God's word, spreading the gospel far and wide, and equipping believers to become effective soul winners. We are committed to nurturing a global community that shares the love of Christ, nurtures faith, and fosters spiritual growth. True Vine Soul Winning Network is dedicated to fulfilling the Great Commission, making disciples of all nations and witnessing the transformative power of the Gospel.

For God so loved the world, that he gave his only Son, that whoever believes in him should not perish but have eternal life. For God did not send his Son into the world to condemn the world, but in order that the world might be saved through him.

John 3:16-17 ESV

Our Values.

Identity in Christ

We help individuals discover and embrace their identity in Christ, recognizing that they are fearfully and wonderfully made in God's image.

Love

Love is at the heart of our mission. We strive to love one another as Christ loved us and to show God's love to the world. Love is the driving force behind our outreach, service, and relationships.

Kingdom Culture

We strive to cultivate a culture that reflects the values and principles of God's kingdom, where love, justice, and righteousness prevail.

Spirit Led Life

We are committed to living a life led by the Holy Spirit, seeking His guidance and empowerment in all that we do.

Faith

We believe in the power of faith to transform lives and bring salvation to the lost. Our unwavering faith in Christ is the foundation of all we do.

Compassion

We have a deep sense of compassion for those who are suffering, lost, or in need. It's through compassion that we reach out to the brokenhearted, the marginalized, and the downtrodden, offering them hope, healing, and the message of salvation.

Unity

We value the unity of believers and strive to foster an atmosphere of harmony and understanding among members. We are one body, diverse in our backgrounds, gifts, and talents, but united in our common faith in Christ.

Excellence

We aim for excellence in all our endeavors. Whether in evangelism, discipleship, or community building, we seek to do all things to the best of our ability, offering God our very best.

Prayer

Prayer is the backbone of our network. We recognize the power of prayer to guide, strengthen, and uplift. We are a community that fervently prays for the lost, for one another, and for the world.

Hope

We are bearers of hope, sharing the message that no one is beyond redemption and that every life has infinite worth. We offer hope to the hopeless and help individuals discover purpose and meaning in Christ.

Generosity

We are generous with our time, resources, and talents. We freely give because we have received God's abundant grace. Generosity is a reflection of God's generosity toward us.


What We Believe.

The Great Commission.

We are committed to fulfilling the Great Commission, as given by Jesus in Matthew 28:19-20, to make disciples of all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father, Son, and Holy Spirit, and teaching them to observe all that Jesus commanded.

Salvation through Jesus Christ.

We believe in the redemptive work of Jesus Christ, who is the only way to salvation. Salvation is a free gift of God's grace, and it is received through faith in Christ's atoning sacrifice on the cross.

The Trinity

We believe in the one true God who exists in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit. These three persons are co-equal, co-eternal, and together make up the triune Godhead.

The Holy Bible as the Word of God.

We believe that the Bible, both the Old and New Testaments, is the inspired and authoritative Word of God. It serves as our guide for faith, practice, and understanding of God's will.

The Return of Christ.

We believe in the second coming of Jesus Christ. His return will be a glorious event, and we eagerly anticipate it as a source of hope and a call to live a life of faithfulness and readiness.
